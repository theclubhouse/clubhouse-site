Pregnancy

Find comfort and relaxation during your pregnancy. I focus on any area that you need: feet and ankles, lower or upper back, shoulders — all of those places that can carry tension during pregnancy.

For the mother's greatest comfort I use soft side-lying cushions.

Massage can help with the baby's position and help prepare your body for labor. After your baby is born, I can help relieve postpartum aches and soreness leaving you relaxed and able to enjoy caring for your precious newborn.

Techniques

I use a variety of techniques to address my clients' needs:

Swedish Massage
Great for reducing muscle stiffness and pain.
Myofascial Release
Stimulation and release of tension and pain in the connective tissue.
Deep Tissue Massage
Relief for the deeper layers of muscle.
Prenatal Massage
A special focus on the needs of an expectant mother.
Postpartum Massage
An excellent way to speed recovery. May also help with postpartum depression.
